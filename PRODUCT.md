# Sivraj Product Context

Sivraj is a memory-first personal AI workspace. Users connect a wallet, create or resume a twin, chat with model-backed memory, manage provider access, and inspect runtime/status surfaces while staying inside one ambient app shell.

## Audience

- Primary users are technical, model-literate builders who care about control, continuity, and fast feedback.
- They scan quickly, often in a dark interface, and expect state to be obvious without explanatory clutter.
- They may be experimenting with providers, local tools, memory, and identity flows, so UI must survive reloads, retries, partial failure, and configuration changes.

## Product Lane

Sivraj is an application, not a marketing site. Design serves the task.

- Prefer dense but calm layouts.
- Make active state, connection state, and failure state explicit.
- Keep controls close to the thing they affect.
- Use drawers, panels, lists, segmented controls, icon buttons, and inline forms where they reduce cognitive load.
- Do not use landing-page tropes, oversized hero copy, decorative feature cards, or marketing scaffolding inside the app.

## Voice

- Calm, precise, direct.
- Slightly warm, never hypey.
- Use specific nouns and verbs: "Connect OpenRouter", "Apply model", "Gemini (default)".
- Do not explain obvious UI mechanics inside the UI.
- Avoid generic SaaS language such as supercharge, empower, seamless, world-class, next-generation, unlock, or streamline.

## Anti-References

- AI-generated SaaS sameness.
- Purple-blue gradients as a default mood.
- Glassmorphism used everywhere without hierarchy.
- Cards inside cards.
- Large icon tiles repeating in grids.
- Long helper copy that repeats labels.
- Tiny low-contrast labels that users must decode.
- Popovers, drawers, and menus clipped by overflow containers.

## Success Criteria

Good Sivraj UI feels like a focused cockpit: compact, luminous, legible, and stateful. It should look intentional at first glance and become easier to use under stress, not just prettier in a screenshot.
